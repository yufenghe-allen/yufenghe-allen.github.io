This study develops the **ICRAFT** platform to identify immunotherapy targets with dual action in tumor cells and T cells.

The project integrates multi-omics data and functional evidence to prioritize targets that may simultaneously enhance tumor sensitivity and T-cell activity. I contributed to **CRISPR screen** data analysis and web development.
