This work integrates **spatial transcriptomics** with multi-omics measurements to characterize tumor microenvironment architecture and prognostic niches in female genital tumors.

The study combines spatial molecular profiling with genomic analysis, including whole-exome sequencing, to understand clinically relevant tissue ecosystems.
