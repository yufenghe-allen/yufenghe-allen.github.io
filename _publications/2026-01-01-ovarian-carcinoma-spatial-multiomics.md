This study constructs a spatial single-cell multi-omics atlas of high-grade serous ovarian carcinoma.

By integrating **spatial transcriptomics**, **spatial proteomics**, and **spatial metabolomics**, the work aims to characterize metastatic trajectories, senescence-associated programs, and immune microenvironment remodeling in ovarian cancer. This project reflects my broader interest in clinically grounded **multimodal** computational biology and translational cancer research.

This work received a Best Poster Award at Cold Spring Harbor Asia.
