This work presents **DISSECT**, a computational framework for integrating cytological images with spatial transcriptomics to improve cell segmentation in complex tissue settings.

Our goal is to achieve more accurate cell boundary delineation and better single-cell resolution in spatial transcriptomic data by combining complementary information from tissue morphology and molecular measurements. More broadly, this work reflects my interest in **deep generative modeling**, **multimodal learning**, and computational methods for high-dimensional biological data integration.

This work was also presented as a poster at the Human Cell Atlas General Meeting.
