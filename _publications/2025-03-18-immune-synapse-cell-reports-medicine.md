This study investigates how re-engaging immune synapse molecules can enhance T-cell cytotoxicity and improve immunotherapy efficacy.

I contributed to the analysis of **whole-exome sequencing** and **methylome** data in this project. More broadly, this work aligns with my interest in tumor immunology and clinically relevant computational analysis.
